Are your spare parts compatible with all elevator brands?

We offer a wide range of elevator spare parts, including motors, buttons, control panels, door systems, guide rails, and safety components. Whether you're looking for mechanical, electrical, or electronic parts, we have you covered.

How can I determine which part is suitable for my elevator?

You can determine the suitable part by checking the elevator model number, manufacturer specifications, or consulting with our technical team who can guide you based on your requirements.

Do you offer original or aftermarket parts?

We offer both original equipment manufacturer (OEM) parts and high-quality aftermarket parts. All our parts come with warranties and meet industry standards.

What is your delivery time?

Delivery time varies based on location and product availability. Typically, we deliver within 3-7 business days for GCC countries and 7-14 days for international orders.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer installation services through our network of certified technicians across various regions. Installation charges may vary based on the complexity of the job.
